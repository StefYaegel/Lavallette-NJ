Welcome to Lavallette

A timeless beach town made of seashells and magic...

Home

For many, this Jersey Shore borough is more than just a summer destination; It's home. Lavallette (or Lava for locals) is a cherished small town full of seafood restaurants, beachwear stores, unique ice cream shops, tiny arcades, big boardwalks, bayside courts, homestyle breakfast spots, coffee shops, and much more.
